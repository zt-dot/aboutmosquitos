Remedies for Bites

Skeeze, Pixabay, Pixabay License
  1. DON'T SCRATCH
    2. Scratching will only make the bite worse
  2. Apply a lotion
    3. Use an anti-itch cream or a calamine lotion to help
  3. Use an ice pack
    4. Something cold with help sooth the bite and itchitness
  4. Use an over the counter anti-histamine
  5. If symptoms are really bad, go to the doctor
    6. If you are vomiting, have a high fever, or vision loss please go to a doctor to receive help.
